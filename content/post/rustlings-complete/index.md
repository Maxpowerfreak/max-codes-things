### Learnings

I write this a little while after being done, and I find I have already mostly forgotten most Rust.

This is not entirely surprising, memory is based on repetition and practice and doing rustlings once every few days or weeks does not constitute enough to trigger retention.

Was my time wasted? Maybe a percentage, but the exercise itself remains valuable. Keeping the brain working is healthy.
